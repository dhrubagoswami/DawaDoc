Inside the app

Built around the person
who actually has to understand it.

Reads messy handwriting

Trained for the notoriously hard-to-read scrawl on Indian prescriptions.

Read aloud

One tap, and the explanation is spoken — for low vision or reading discomfort.

Dosage timing, made obvious

Before food, after food, morning, night — visual, not buried in jargon.

Side effects to watch for

A short, relevant list — not an overwhelming package insert.

Scan history

Signed-in users get past scans grouped by date — nothing to dig up twice.

Hindi or English

Every explanation available in simple Hindi or English — switch anytime.

No friction

Works instantly.
No account required.

  • Scan and read a prescription right away — zero setup.
  • Sign in with Google only if you want scan history saved.
  • Without sign-in, nothing is stored — the photo is analyzed and discarded.
  • Always honest about its limits — it explains the label, it doesn't replace a doctor.
